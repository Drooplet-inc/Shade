Introduction

Shade is the perfect multifunction bot to spice up your server. The bot is community driven and can also be developed with your help.

About this Bot

The bot provides several features such as moderation, information, fun and many more! The bot is hosted 24/7 and you can be reassured about its availability.

Technology

The bot is built with JavaScript using the discord.js library, similarly with the API that the bot uses.

The bot uses its own API to filter and deliver high quality fun and useful commands to your server. With the help of our super fast and flexible API, we are able to provide you with the best and fastest features.

Getting Started

To get started with our bot is fairly easy and we have done our best to make it simpler for you.

In-bot help

You also have the option to get help on all the bot commands right through discord with the use of the bot's internal help command.

Important: You must agree to our usage policy and the question about it will be quite strict.
Use s!policy for more information.

Adding the bot

To add the bot to your server click here.

This does needs you to either be the owner of the server or have "manage server" permissions.
